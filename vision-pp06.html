PP06

Collingwood’s Voice at Simcoe County

Regional stewardship. Local results.

Why Simcoe County Matters

Many services affecting everyday life in Collingwood are planned, funded or delivered through Simcoe County. Strong representation helps ensure Collingwood’s interests are understood while contributing to sound regional decisions.

Regional Stewardship

Serving on County Council is about representing Collingwood while helping make decisions that strengthen Simcoe County as a whole. Strong regional decisions ultimately benefit local communities.

Guiding Principle: Listen to Collingwood. Advocate for Collingwood. Build consensus across Simcoe County. Make decisions that strengthen both the County and our community.

01

Understand the Mayor’s Role

The Mayor advocates for Collingwood while serving as a member of Simcoe County Council, exercising independent judgement in the interests of the municipality and its constituents.

02

Bring Relevant Experience

Municipal service, engineering work across Simcoe County, upper-tier infrastructure management and leadership with AMO and OSUM inform a collaborative, evidence-based approach.

03

Learn From Neighbours

County Council provides opportunities to learn from neighbouring communities and adapt successful ideas to Collingwood where appropriate.

Vision for Collingwood — Publication No. 6: Collingwood’s Voice at Simcoe County.

Understanding the County Role

Regional services and decisions that shape everyday life in Collingwood

Understanding the County Role

Infrastructure & Emergency Services

County roads and paramedic services require close coordination between Simcoe County and local municipalities.

Housing & Community Supports

Affordable and supportive housing, homelessness programs and long-term care are County responsibilities with direct local impact.

Planning & Regional Prosperity

Waste management, regional planning and economic development benefit from practical collaboration and a clear Collingwood voice.

The Mayor’s County Role

  1. Represent Bring Collingwood’s needs and perspectives to County Council.
  2. Judge Independently Consider evidence and the interests of the municipality served.
  3. Collaborate Build productive relationships with County leaders and fellow councillors.
  4. Coordinate Strengthen communication between County and Town Council.
  5. Steward Support sound regional decisions that benefit local communities.

Key County Responsibilities

Roads & Paramedics

Regional transportation infrastructure and emergency medical response.

Housing & Homelessness

Affordable and supportive housing and programs addressing homelessness.

Long-Term Care & Waste

Services that support residents and responsible regional waste management.

Planning & Economy

Regional planning and economic development across Simcoe County.

Role context: Municipal Act, 2001; Ontario Municipal Councillor’s Guide; Simcoe County service responsibilities.

The Approach

Strong advocacy, practical partnerships and responsible regional stewardship

The Approach

01

Listen to Collingwood

Keep residents, businesses and Town Council informed and ensure local priorities shape County advocacy.

02

Build County Relationships

Develop productive working relationships with County leadership and fellow councillors.

03

Review Strategic Priorities

Understand County plans, budgets and priorities that affect Collingwood and the wider region.

04

Identify Shared Opportunities

Find practical areas where County and local action can deliver stronger outcomes together.

05

Strengthen Communication

Improve the flow of information between County Council, Town Council and the community.

06

Adapt Proven Ideas

Learn from neighbouring municipalities and bring successful approaches home where they fit Collingwood.

Decision Test for County Priorities

Local Voice

Are Collingwood’s interests and residents’ needs clearly understood?

Regional Benefit

Will the decision strengthen Simcoe County and its local communities?

Evidence

Is the position supported by sound information and practical analysis?

Collaboration

Does it create opportunities for municipalities to work together?

Stewardship

Does it use public resources responsibly and support long-term results?

Connecting the Vision

Policy Position

Collingwood needs strong County representation grounded in local advocacy, independent judgement, evidence-based decisions and responsible regional stewardship.
